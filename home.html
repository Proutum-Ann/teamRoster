Hello! Welcome to my character roster site! This site specifically contains my characters from various ARPGs that I participate in on ToyHouse!

Pokemon Mystery Dungeon: Destruction Call

Destruction Call is a PMD ARPG focused on keeping true to the integrity of the PMD series as much as possible! It explores the world of Kogranar, a world shaped like a closed flower. Though it looks peaceful, it hides a lot of ancient history.
Follow the story to find out about it!

Silent Eclipse Redux

Silent Eclipse Redux is a rerun of another discontinued ARPG: Silent Eclipse. The world follows Pokemon in a Warrior Cats setting.
